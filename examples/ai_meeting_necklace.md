# Friend - AI Wearable Meeting Assistant

## Product Overview

Friend is a revolutionary AI-powered wearable necklace designed for busy professionals who attend multiple meetings daily. The device uses advanced AI to automatically record, transcribe, and summarize every conversation, allowing users to focus on the discussion rather than taking notes.

## Key Features

### Always-On Recording

- 24/7 automatic recording capability
- Smart activation when voices are detected
- No need to manually start/stop recording
- Ultra-long battery life (48 hours continuous use)

### AI-Powered Transcription

- Real-time speech-to-text conversion
- Multi-language support (15+ languages)
- Speaker identification and labeling
- 95%+ accuracy even in noisy environments

### Intelligent Summarization

- Automatic generation of meeting summaries
- Key action items and decision extraction
- Topic categorization and tagging
- Sentiment analysis of conversations

### Seamless Integration

- Syncs with calendar apps (Google Calendar, Outlook, Apple Calendar)
- Direct integration with Slack, Teams, and email
- CRM integration (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- Export to note-taking apps (Notion, Evernote, OneNote)

### Privacy & Security

- End-to-end encryption for all recordings
- GDPR and HIPAA compliant
- User-controlled data retention policies
- Opt-in sharing with explicit permissions

## Technical Specifications

- **Processor**: Custom AI chip with 16 TOPS performance
- **Storage**: 128GB internal storage (stores 10,000+ hours)
- **Connectivity**: Bluetooth 5.3, Wi-Fi 6E, LTE (optional)
- **Battery**: 2000mAh lithium polymer, 48h continuous use
- **Charging**: USB-C fast charging (0-100% in 2 hours)
- **Weight**: 35 grams
- **Water Resistance**: IP67 rated
- **Microphones**: 4-array beamforming mics with noise cancellation
